Usage:

  html2txt <input-file>
      Converts the HTML document in the <input-file> to plain text, and writes
      it to STDOUT.
  html2txt <input-file> <output-file>
      Converts the HTML document in the <input-file> to plain text, and writes
      it to the <output-file>.
  html2txt -help
      Prints this text.
